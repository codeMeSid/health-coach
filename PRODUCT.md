# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Solo user. Opens the page at home (kitchen counter or desk) under warm indoor light, often mid-cook or pre-gym. Job: find today’s meal or workout, read calories/protein/steps clearly, act without hunting.

## Product Purpose

Health Map is a personal weekly meal and workout viewer. Vegetarian, egg-inclusive, GERD-friendly meals plus a 5-day workout plan. Success: today’s food and training visible in seconds, numbers readable at arm’s length, no chrome between user and plan.

## Brand Personality

Absurdist food-cartoon noir on a chaotic scrapboard. Corkboard dossier energy: rubber stamps, washi tape, polaroid clips, hard-boiled kitchen jokes. Precise about the plan facts; unserious about the costume.

Stamp packs (lottery + Stamp drawer): **110** skins in `stamp-packs.mjs` — scrapboard, genre chaos, type homage, plus **rubber-hose / 1920s–30s cartoon homage** (`hose-good`, `hose-evil`, `hose-ink`, `hose-loop`, `hose-glove`, `hose-noir`, `hose-farm`, `hose-chase`, `hose-toon`, `hose-frame`). Homage + parody labels only — no Looney Tunes / studio character names or art. Tokens via JS; free Google Fonts (OFL).

Rubber-hose packs (`fx=hose`) add ink page wash + grain; other packs stay kraft.

## Anti-references

- MyFitnessPal-style neon clutter and dense metric walls
- Gym-bro / high-contrast fitness-bro aesthetics
- Clinical EHR gray form grids
- Identical icon + heading + blurb card grids
- Tiny uppercase eyebrows on every section
- Soft glassmorphism / neumorphism as the default material language
- Calm “kitchen journal” tastefulness that hides the gags

## Design Principles

1. Info before chrome — content hierarchy wins; absurdity coats the frame, not the calories
2. One job per view — meal or workout, not both fighting for attention
3. Bold type hierarchy — larger body, clear weight steps, arm’s-length readable stamp numbers
4. Scrap with intention — corkboard collage, not random chaos; lottery changes paint only
5. Numbers earn space — macros and targets scannable in every stamp pack

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Prefer larger body type than typical web defaults. Body and UI text contrast ≥4.5:1 (large text ≥3:1). Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`. Color is never the sole cue for tabs or status. Stamp lottery never changes meaning or which day is active.
